# Scenario Catalog

## Design Rules

Scenarios must feel like real driving, not minigames. Each scenario has a route context, trigger conditions, NPC behaviour envelope, success criteria, failure conditions, telemetry events, and instructor override controls.

## Core Scenarios

### Uncontrolled Intersection

- Context: failed traffic lights or missing right-of-way clarity.
- Hazards: pedestrians entering late, impatient kombi, cross traffic, blocked sightline.
- Skills: observation, yielding, speed control, clutch/brake control, indicator use.

### Informal Kombi Stop

- Context: kombi stops at non-designated pickup point.
- Hazards: passengers stepping into road, sudden door opening, trailing vehicle pressure.
- Skills: anticipation, following distance, lane positioning, safe overtaking decision.

### Pothole Avoidance

- Context: degraded tarmac or murram transition.
- Hazards: oncoming traffic, hidden puddle depth, tyre damage, abrupt swerving.
- Skills: scan distance, speed reduction, smooth steering, lane discipline.

### Police Roadblock

- Context: ZRP Traffic compliance checkpoint.
- Hazards: queue compression, officer hand signals, temporary cones/signs, sudden stop.
- Skills: compliance, speed reduction, stopping position, document-check flow, calm restart.

### Overloaded Vehicle Interaction

- Context: freight, pickup, bus, or agricultural vehicle with unstable load.
- Hazards: poor acceleration, falling debris, wide turning, braking distance.
- Skills: hazard spacing, overtaking judgement, patience, route planning.

### Night Driving

- Context: inconsistent street lighting and load-shedding darkness.
- Hazards: pedestrians in low contrast, unlit vehicles, glare, broken markings.
- Skills: headlight use, speed adaptation, scanning, lane positioning.

### Wet-Season Flooding

- Context: heavy rain, standing water, reduced visibility.
- Hazards: hydroplaning, hidden potholes, flooded low crossing, poor braking.
- Skills: speed selection, wiper/light use, following distance, route abort decision.

### Livestock And Wildlife

- Context: rural or peri-urban roads.
- Hazards: unpredictable animal movement, herders, narrow shoulders.
- Skills: speed control, horn judgement, gentle braking, escape path planning.

### School Zone

- Context: opening or closing time near school.
- Hazards: children crossing unpredictably, parked vehicles, vendors, buses.
- Skills: speed compliance, scanning, stopping readiness, pedestrian priority.

## Adaptive Difficulty

Difficulty should adjust through traffic density, visibility, weather, NPC assertiveness, road degradation, route complexity, and instructor-injected incidents. The learner should never feel ambushed; harder scenarios should still be plausible.

## Scenario Acceptance Checklist

- Has a Zimbabwe-specific reason to exist.
- Uses believable road users and vehicles.
- Records clear telemetry events.
- Has instructor controls.
- Can be replayed for debrief.
- Maps to one or more assessment categories.
